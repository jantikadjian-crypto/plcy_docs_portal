# Policy packs & enforcement

_The Control ⊂ Primitive ⊂ Composite model and enforcement modes._

Policy is composed hierarchically: Controls are the atomic checks, Primitives bundle related controls, and Composite packs assemble primitives into deployable, market-ready bundles (e.g. HIPAA, Financial Services, EU AI Act).

## Enforcement

- Monitor — observe and log without blocking.

- Warn — flag and annotate the request.

- Block — stop the request before sensitive data leaves.

Enforcement Controls and the Policy Editor manage how packs apply; the catalog is the source of truth referenced across the console.
