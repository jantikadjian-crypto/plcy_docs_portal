# Pricing & the quote builder

_The plans catalog, add-ons with bulk packs, discount levers, and live quotes._

Pricing & Plans is the catalog: plans (capacity + entitlements), add-ons with discounted bulk packs, global discount levers, named throughput tiers, and a Bedrock model catalog.

## Quote builder

- Pick a plan, billing cadence, and term; the plan’s capacity pre-fills the Demand fields.

- Overages are priced by the cheaper of unit vs pack; cache hits reduce billable requests.

- Toggle entitlements (immutable logs, HITL, Bedrock, custom models), add a Dedicated Cloud tier + region, and export the order form as CSV/Markdown.

## Reconciliation

Existing customers map to catalog plans by tier (Growth → Team, Trial → Builder). Each plan card shows customers on it and their MRR; a customer’s Billing tab shows its catalog plan, entitlements, and list-vs-actual MRR.
