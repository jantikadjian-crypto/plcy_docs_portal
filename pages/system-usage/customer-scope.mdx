# Customer scope: fleet vs single customer

_Switch between a fleet-wide view and a single customer to re-scope pages and reports._

The customer switcher (top of the sidebar) controls the scope of scope-aware surfaces. With "All Customers" selected you see the whole fleet; pick a customer to narrow everything to that account.

## What re-scopes

- Reports (Fleet Posture, SLA, Cost & Margin, Incident, DSAR) generate for the selected scope.

- Cost & Margin, SLA, and Data Requests filter to the customer.

- Setting scope from a search result or a customer page follows you across the app.
