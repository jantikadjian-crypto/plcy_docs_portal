# Data retention & residency

_How long data is kept, where it lives, and how transfers are governed._

## Retention

Log and evidence retention is set per plan (from 7 days on Free up to ~3 years on Enterprise Cloud) and in org settings. Retention windows are visible on each plan and in the customer’s deployment configuration.

## Residency

- Region selection pins data to a jurisdiction; Sovereign Cloud and air-gapped options exist for strict requirements.

- Cross-border transfers are governed on the Data Transfers and Residency Controls pages.

- Sub-processors are tracked and disclosed on the Sub-processors page.
