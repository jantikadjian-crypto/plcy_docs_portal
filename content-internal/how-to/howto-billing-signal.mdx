# Respond to a billing signal

_Handle a failed payment, dunning, or dispute routed to on-call._

Failed payments, dunning, and disputes route into Notifications and page on-call. Work them from Billing Health.

## Steps

1. From the Notifications alert (or Billing Health), open the flagged customer and invoice.

2. Identify the signal — failed payment, dunning stage, or dispute / chargeback.

3. Take the matching action: retry or update the payment method, contact the customer, or contest a dispute with evidence.

4. Update the invoice / account status to reflect the resolution.

5. Confirm the signal clears and no further escalation is queued.

Tip: repeated dunning without resolution escalates toward suspension — act before it reaches the final stage.
