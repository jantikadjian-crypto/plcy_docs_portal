# Handle a data-subject request (DSAR)

_Work a DSAR from intake to fulfilment before its statutory deadline._

DSARs are tracked against a statutory clock. The goal is to fulfil (access, export, or delete) before the deadline and leave an evidence trail.

## Steps

1. Open Data Requests and select the incoming request.

2. Verify the requester’s identity and confirm the data subject and jurisdiction.

3. Assign an owner and note the statutory deadline.

4. Determine the request type — access, export, rectification, or erasure — and gather or action the data.

5. Record the outcome and mark the request fulfilled before the deadline.

6. Check the DSAR Fulfilment report to confirm it scores On track / Closed rather than At risk / Breached.
