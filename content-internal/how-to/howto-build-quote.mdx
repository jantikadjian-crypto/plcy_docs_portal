# Build and export a quote

_Price a deal in the quote builder and export the order form._

The quote builder prices a plan plus usage, options, and discounts, and produces an order form you can export.

## Steps

1. Open Pricing & Plans and switch to the Quote tab.

2. Pick a plan, a billing cadence (monthly / annual), and a term.

3. Set the Demand fields — governed requests and seats; the plan’s capacity pre-fills them.

4. Set Options — throughput tier, Dedicated Cloud tier + region, cache-hit rate, and BYOK vs PLCY-managed model access.

5. Toggle entitlements on or off; apply any discount levers.

6. Review the live total and the per-line breakdown (overages price at the cheaper of unit vs pack).

7. Export the order form as CSV or Markdown.

Tip: the quote reconciles to real customers — each plan card shows who’s on it and their MRR.
