# Change a deployment (region or size)

_Raise a provisioning change-request and schedule it into a maintenance window._

Region, size, and connectivity changes are handled as reviewed change-requests scheduled into a maintenance window — never applied ad hoc. You need the provision.manage capability.

## Steps

1. Open the customer (or Provisioning) and start a configuration change-request.

2. Choose the target — new region, size tier (Small / Medium / Large), or connectivity.

3. Submit the request; it enters the review queue with a diff of what changes.

4. Review and approve the change-request.

5. Schedule it into a maintenance window on SLA & Maintenance so the customer has notice.

6. The change executes in the window; confirm the deployment reflects the new config afterward.
